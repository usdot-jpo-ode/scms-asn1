107 ---
108 -- @brief A certificate store is a list of root and elector endorsements plus
109 -- lists of ICA and PCA certificates needed by EEs to validate message.
110 -- This base type is used to construct the GCCF and the LCCF. The PG
111 -- creates the GCCF by assembling all certificates needed to validate
112 -- all EE certificates in the SCMS. An RA creates the LCCF by pruning
113 -- the GCCF down to the lists needed by the EEs that are assigned to
114 -- that RA. Note that these are lists of certificates, not certificate
115 -- chains as defined in 1609.2. The primary reason for not using a list
116 -- of certificate chains is to eliminate redundancy. If we used a list
117 -- of certificate chains, each chain would have a copy of the same root
118 -- CA and some chains would have copies of the same ICA.
119 -- @class CertificateStore
120 -- @param rootCAEndorsements are optional for as long as the SCMS OPs project
121 -- does not have Electors. As soon as they are
122 -- introduced, a minimum of 1 rootCAEndorsement is
123 -- required.
124 -- @param electorEndorsements are required by the SCMS components (including
125 -- EEs) to validate non-expired certificates before
126 -- adding a new Elector and storing new elector
127 -- certificate in the certificate store.
128 -- @param maCertificate is the MA certificate.
129 -- @param certs is a list of cerificates of all other SCMS
130 -- components.
131 -- @see Certificate
132 CertificateStore ::= SEQUENCE {
133  rootCAEndorsements SEQUENCE SIZE(0..MAX) OF ElectorBallot,
134  electorEndorsements SEQUENCE SIZE(0..MAX) OF ElectorBallot,
135  maCertificate Certificate,
136  certs SEQUENCE SIZE(0..MAX) OF Certificate,
137  ...
138 }
